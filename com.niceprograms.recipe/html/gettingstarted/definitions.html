Definitions

  • Title - The title of the recipe (e.g. Bean Soup).
  • Cuisine - The style of cooking (e.g. Italian, French, Canadian, Indian)
  • Category - Used for organizing recipes (e.g. Breakfast, Dinner, Snack, Vegetarian)
  • Ingredients - The building blocks of the recipe.
  • Directions - Instructions on preparing and creating the recipe.
